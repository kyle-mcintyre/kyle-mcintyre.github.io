This past summer I interned for JPMorgan Chase as a software engineer in their Enterprise Computer Services division. I worked on a team in an agile enviroment to develop a web application. Over the ten weeks, I was able to develop my technical skils by creating the server-side program to convert information about the company's mainframe environemt into schematic diagrams. During the project I also learned how to create a database for easy scalability and longevity. Within the workplace I also collaborated with several othe teams across many time zones to bundle the project into a functioning web application. This experience has greatly helped me advance my software skills and improve my business skills.

I gave several presentations to senior leaders to discuss my ideas and recommendations for the direction of the chat bot project and vendor selection. Through my internship I developed teamwork and communication skills.
